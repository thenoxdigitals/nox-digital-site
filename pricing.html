How pricing works

Nox AI uses a credit-based model. You pay for access (1 month, 12 months, or 3 years) and receive a monthly allowance of Nox credits. Each time you generate a full website, you spend 1 credit.

This keeps your costs predictable while still giving you the flexibility to use more credits when you need them.

What happens if I run out of credits?

If you hit 0 credits, you’ll be prompted to wait for your next monthly refresh or purchase additional credit packs (coming soon).

Can I change plans later?

You can upgrade from monthly to yearly or 3‑year plans in the future. Upgrades will prorate based on what you’ve already paid.

Do credits roll over?

For launch, unused credits do not roll over. This keeps the pricing simple. Rollover may be added later for higher tiers.
