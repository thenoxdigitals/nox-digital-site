Frequently asked questions.

How long does a website project take?

Most Starter sites launch in 1–2 weeks. Growth and Premium projects usually take 3–6 weeks depending on content and feedback speed.

Do you help with content and images?

Yes. We guide you on what to write, help refine your copy, and suggest image styles. We can also work with your existing photos.

Do I need to buy hosting and domain?

We’ll advise you on domain options and hosting. If you already have them, we will connect everything for you.

Will my website appear on Google?

We set up basic SEO and submit your site to Google. Ranking depends on competition and content, but we design with search in mind.

Can you redesign my current website?

Yes. We can migrate content, keep what works, and rebuild the design and structure for better performance.

Do you offer ongoing support?

We offer support plans for updates, new pages, and growth work. Or you can request changes as needed.

Can we pay in parts?

Most projects use a 50% upfront / 50% on launch model. For bigger projects we can break this into more milestones.

What tools and platforms do you use?

We pick tools based on your needs — usually modern stacks that are easy to maintain and fast to load.

Not sure which plan fits?

Tell us about your business and we’ll suggest a realistic budget and plan in plain language.
